FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a project typically take?

Starter projects are usually done in 3–5 days. Growth packages take 7–14 days. Elite full-stack systems typically take 2–4 weeks depending on scope and how fast you provide feedback.

Do I need to already have GoHighLevel?

Yes, you'll need an active GHL account (or a sub-account under an agency). If you don't have one yet, I can guide you on the best plan for your needs during the discovery call.

What if I need changes after launch?

All packages include a revision round before final delivery. Elite packages include 30 days of post-launch support. Beyond that, I offer affordable retainer arrangements for ongoing work.

Can you work with my existing GHL setup?

Absolutely. I can audit, clean up, and build on top of existing GHL setups. I'll review what you have and let you know the best path forward in our discovery call.
